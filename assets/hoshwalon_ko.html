Some words:
  • 'hosh walo' means-people who are in their full sense.
  • 'bekhudi' is the opposite state of the above, senselessness. Here, It means a passionate
    • state where being in Love deeply with someone you are totally unaware of all other things
    going on around you - everything else seems immaterial to you. Means, One can't fathom
    the bliss of such a joyful state unless he or she is so much lost in love to lose sense of
    everything else around him or her.
  • fizaa - the vast space / or atmosphere for smaller purposes :p
  • mausam - season
  • shayari - poetry
  • haal-e-dil - state of heart
